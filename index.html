About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistical Sciences at the University of Toronto, advised by Stanislav Volgushev and Lei Sun. Previously, I completed an M.Phil. in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science at the University of Hong Kong, and a B.Sc. in Information and Computing Science at Harbin Institute of Technology.

Research

My current research focuses on the theoretical and computational aspects of nonparametric mixture models, with an application emphasis on empirical Bayes.
